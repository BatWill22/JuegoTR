Main controls:
- Move forward: "d"
- Move backward: "a"
- Jump: space
- Attack: left mouse
- Dash: left shift
- Look up: "w"
- Look down: "s"


Additional information:
- you can attack up or down by attacking while looking at the desired direction
- you can open doors by attacking
- you can only open red, green or blue doors if you have collected the key of each color
